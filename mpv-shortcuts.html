Essential Tips & Best Practices

Quick Start Guide:

  1. Start with CTRL+3 (Ravu-Zoom) for general content
  2. For anime, try CTRL+4 (Ani4k) first
  3. For HD movies, use CTRL+1 (FSRCNNX)
  4. For SD content, use CTRL+2 (FSRCNNX+) or CTRL+5 (AniSD for anime)
  5. Press 'c' to clear shaders and compare with original

Performance Tips:

  • If experiencing lag, switch to lighter profiles (CTRL+1, CTRL+3)
  • High-end GPUs can handle NNEDI3 (CTRL+7/8) and Anime4K (CTRL+6)
  • Consider disabling debanding (F2 cycles through off/medium/strong)
  • Use 'q' to quit - it saves your position automatically

Troubleshooting:

  • No video? Try changing gpu-api from vulkan to opengl in mpv.conf
  • Subtitles not showing? Check sub/, subs/, subtitles/ folders
  • Audio issues? F1 toggles loudness normalization
  • Remember: Lines starting with # in config files are disabled
